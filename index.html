Game Features
Explore the new world!
The Imps mined all the treasures in their old world and opened a portal to a new one, which they called the IMP's Kingdom. In the new world, you have to hunt for treasures, find powerful artifacts, pump your heroes, fight frightening bosses and build your own kingdom!
Hero
Your Heroes are your pride! Each of your Heroes uses a unique fighting style, weapons, and abilities. Send heroes on quests, let them serve your purposes by fighting for the most epic treasures!
Boss raids
Take part in battles against powerful creatures that threaten your kingdoms! The fight with them will be incredibly difficult, but the winners will receive high-level rewards!
Army
The army is an obligatory attribute of any kingdom! Create a huge legion under the leadership of powerful Generals and conquer the castles of your enemies! Put in place those who dare to challenge you or stand in the way of the desired goal!
Castle
The castle is the foundation of any kingdom, it is a demonstration of power, glory, and property. Thanks to your own castle, you will be able to produce exclusive goods: artifacts, weapons, armor, etc.
Clan wars
Unite with allies in clans. Seize the territory of rivals, put the impudent in their place, or incite others to conflicts for your own benefit. Anything for the sake of being the best.

Collect treasures
An incredible amount of valuable and unique treasures await in the new world of imps! Clear dungeons, explore locations, and go through many quests in search of resources, equipment, and great artifacts that can instantly increase your power!
Trading
Rewards, resources, unique equipment ... this is all good, but it could be even better! Thanks to the in-game exchange, you can make money with everything you have! Absolutely any property can be offered to be redeemed by other players at the price tag set by you personally! Get rid of unnecessary items, speculate or find the best deals on the market yourself!
Farm
Earn our tokens just by playing the game, become the richest owner of IMP tokens!
NFT marketplace
Every artifact, piece of equipment, and even a hero is an NFT. Sell your items or buy valuables from other Players at the in-game auction for IMP tokens.
LP Staking
We will support people from the community who will make LP Staking, rewarding them with tokens and very rare in-game items that cannot be obtained in any other way.
